About me

I am a security researcher at Thalium.

I specialize in reverse engineering and vulnerability research. I am interested in low level security, fuzzing, binary exploitation techniques and mitigations. I also enjoy cryptography.

Previously, I have played CTFs with SHRECS, a team that I created with fellow students of my university. During that time, we helped promoting the CTF culture in CentraleSupélec, and won multiple competitions such as Imperial CTF, HackDay, THCon CTF and Orange CTF.

I have also been part of ECSC Team France for four years in a row, including when we finished 3rd place in European Cyber Security Challenge 2022 (Vienna edition).

You can hit me up on Discord @face0xff.
